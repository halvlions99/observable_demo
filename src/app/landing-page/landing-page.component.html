Welcome to Smoke House Grill

This is a simple demo application for educational purpose. This small demo will show the power of RxJS, NGRX and Observables. Observables provide powerful, precise and less error prone streams of data. I have also added the simple architecture of one-way dataflow (smart & dumb components). I will demo an alternative to NGRX using ‘Service with a Subject’ combined with RxJS.
